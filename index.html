About Me

I'm a Data Analyst and aspiring Data Scientist, recently graduated from UC Santa Cruz with a B.S. in Mathematics — Theory and Computation (Minor in Computer Science & Statistics). My work sits at the intersection of rigorous mathematical thinking and hands-on engineering.

When I'm not wrangling data, I'm probably exploring new model architectures, working on side projects, or getting lost in a math textbook.
