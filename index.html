About Me

I'm a Data Analyst and aspiring Data Scientist, recently graduated from UC Santa Cruz with a B.S. in Mathematics — Theory and Computation (Minor in Computer Science & Statistics). My work sits at the intersection of rigorous mathematical thinking and hands-on engineering.

Across three internships — a marketing analytics agency, an HR consulting firm, and a public utility — I've built real production systems: AI-powered NLP pipelines, interactive Streamlit dashboards, dbt data models on GCP BigQuery, and ML models predicting employee churn.

I'm fluent in Python and SQL, comfortable with the full analytics stack from ETL to executive-ready visualization, and increasingly focused on applying LLMs and NLP to automate workflows that used to require hours of human judgment.

When I'm not wrangling data, I'm probably exploring new model architectures, working on side projects, or getting lost in a math textbook.
